# Version 1 — Name Pools

Pre-generated names for all 11 species. Loaded into `NamePool` by
`scripts/seed_names.py` at game initialisation. The simulation draws from
these pools at runtime; when a pool is exhausted it falls back to structured
codes.

Names are designed to feel phonetically distinct per species. Future culture
modules can replace or extend these pools without changing the generator
interface.

---
